About the project



The goal of the project is to design an intuitive and user-friendly mobile app that helps construction professionals manage their projects, communicate with their team, and access important information on the job site.

The app will feature a dashboard that gives users an overview of their current projects, including details such as the project location, budget, and timeline. Users will also be able to view and update project documents, such as blueprints and contracts, as well as communicate with their team through in-app messaging and file sharing.

In addition, the app will include a toolkit section with features such as a calculator for estimating materials and a converter for imperial to metric measurements. The app will also provide access to a library of resources such as industry articles and safety guidelines.

The UI/UX design of the app was focused on creating a clean and straightforward interface that allows users to easily navigate between different sections and access the information they need. The design will also prioritize the use of clear and concise language to ensure that the app is accessible to users with a range of technical expertise.
