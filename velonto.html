About the project



The Velonto platform aims to provide a convenient and efficient delivery service for busy working individuals, while also promoting sustainability. The user interface (UI) is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, allowing users to quickly and easily place orders for meals and parcels. The platform prioritizes speed and efficiency, with a focus on zero CO2 emissions, making it a eco-friendly way of delivery.

In terms of user experience (UX), the focus is on providing a seamless and stress-free ordering process. The platform uses intuitive design elements, such as clear call-to-action buttons and simple forms, to make it easy for users to place orders and track the status of their deliveries. Additionally, the platform may offer features such as real-time delivery tracking, personalized recommendations, and the ability to schedule deliveries in advance, to further enhance the user experience.
