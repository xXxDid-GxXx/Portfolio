About the project



OpenInvest is a web-based investment platform that aims to provide a user-friendly and intuitive interface for users to manage their portfolios. The platform has a clean and modern design, with a focus on simplicity and ease of use.

The homepage of the website prominently displays a login button and a call to action to sign up for an account. Once logged in, users are taken to their dashboard, where they can view their portfolio and performance, as well as access tools for researching and managing their investments.

The navigation bar at the top of the page allows users to easily access different sections of the platform, such as the research center, where they can find information on different stocks and funds, and the trade center, where they can place trades and manage their positions.

The user interface is designed to be responsive and optimized for both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring a seamless experience across different devices. Overall, OpenInvest's UI/UX design is focused on providing a simple and efficient investment experience for its users.
